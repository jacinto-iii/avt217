Design Brief

1. Problem Identification and definiton:

The need is for the creation of a portfolio that markets the artist as professional, serviceable, and innovative. There are many freelance artists out there and the purpose of creating a well structured portfolio is to separate from our competition and leave the client not wanting but needing our services.

2. Goals:

The goal for this portfolio is to generate a high-volume amount of visitors that could become potential clients. For the design, the goal is to be simple for the viewer to browse, appealing to the eye and creatively structured to differentiate the artist from other portfolios.

3. Target Audience:

Advertising agencies, high-profile clients and local small businesses.

4. Positioning strategies:

Through research, we will be able to create a website portfolio that will touch the richness of great design through the endless list of capabilities that HTML and CSS have to offer.

5. Deliverables:

A fully functioning HTML website with an external stylesheet that will consistent throughout most modern browsers.
